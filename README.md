# HunterConnect

HunterConnect is a full-stack web application built for Hunter College students to discover and join campus clubs, share announcements, and stay connected with their college community.

## Purpose

Hunter College clubs often post their events and updates across different social media platforms, making it difficult for students to keep track of everything alongside other content they follow. HunterConnect brings all clubs together in one dedicated platform where they can post their events and announcements, and students can easily stay informed about the clubs they care about.
